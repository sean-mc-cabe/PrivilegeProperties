Our Uniqueness

My name is Lisa Privilege and we are delighted to introduce you to... PRIVILEGE PROPERTIES, North Belfast's newest Estate Agency.

We may be a new company, but, in our team, we have over 60 years' experience to help us achieve our aims...and over 50% of that time has been spent in North Belfast!!

Our brand has been designed to represent our traditional core values. Our colours are simply 'black and white', because we don't believe that there should be any need for 'grey areas' in the business. We will ALWAYS be open, honest and transparent.

We aim to offer the best of both worlds, combining good old fashioned, one to one service along with the benefits of modern day technology.

Our commitment is to put our clients' best interests first and to give independent and objective advice at all times. We understand that appropriate and timely communication is vital and so we undertake to have regular and meaningful updates, in person, on the telephone or by text or e mail as each client prefers.

Costs and risks will always be fully discussed and explained and our clients can be assured of our absolute confidentiality. We will also adhere strictly to all professional regulations and industry requirements and best practice.
