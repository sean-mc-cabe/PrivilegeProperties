Our Services

Valuations

Privilege Properties is happy to provide FREE, Sales and Rental valuations, at a time to suit you.

If you are thinking of moving house or have just purchased an investment property, the first, most important step is to find out what the property is worth....not some inflated and overly optimistic figure, but a genuine, realistic valuation on which you can base your sums. We are happy to discuss all aspects of a prospective move and, in addition, very often, can help with advice on the property in which you are interested.

We will, of course, also discuss, in full, the cost implications of any decisions you may make. It's all part of the service!

Please note that there may be a small fee due for professional, written valuations, say for a bank, or for a solicitor for matrimonial purposes.

Rental

Whether an investor has one rented property, or a sizeable portfolio, it represents a major financial commitment.

Our responsibility is to maximise the return on that investment, only one element of which is making sure that we obtain the maximum rent possible. In many ways, almost more importantly, it is key that we find THE RIGHT TENANT, someone who will look after the property as if it was their own and who can meet their financial responsibilities.

We carry out extensive background checks on all potential tenants, as required by law and by good, simple, common sense....where possible, we check their financial background, their employment history and any previous rental experience. Very simply, if we wouldn't be happy letting a property we hypothetically own to a particular tenant, then we won't let them into a property you own.

We make sure that all tenants are fully aware of the commitments they are making and the responsibilities they are taking on. We also take from them a 'security deposit', which can be forfeited if they do not comply with the terms of their lease...and everything is lodged with the Tenancy Deposit Scheme, to make sure that everyone's interests are legally protected.

Of course, for investors, getting the correct property in the first place is vitally important, which is why we are happy to offer advice on any property they are interested in buying and to introduce contractors who can do any remedial work which is required. It's all part of the service!

Management

Many landlords, understandably, want very little to do with the day to day detail surrounding their property and their tenancy. They may be living overseas, or maybe they're very busy in other aspects of their lives and, if so, what they want and need is an agent they can trust to look after ALL the many different elements of what makes up a successful investment....and that's where we come in!

Our service has been specifically designed to ensure that we tick every conceivable box....from checking the tenant in to the property, with the appropriate completion of an inventory form, to the collection of the rent, to chasing rent which is overdue, to fielding maintenance calls and arranging for any issues to be dealt with promptly, where possible, by one of our range of local North Belfast contractors. We make regular inspection visits to the property, to make sure that the tenant is looking after it as we would expect ....and we have a 24 hour 'help line' for emergencies.

We pride ourselves on attention to detail and compliance with every legal requirement is a given, plus we can advise on any changes in legislation BEFORE they happen.

We also believe that communication is hugely important and so we undertake to keep in touch with every landlord client as often as they require and in the manner in which they find it most convenient, be that face to face, over the telephone or via text or e mail. It's all part of the service!

Residential Sales

We believe that our extensive experience in the residential market, especially in North Belfast, enables us to provide extensive, in depth information on all aspects of a potential move and to do so with empathy and in the strictest confidence.

Key to our strategy is to ensure that the advice we provide is geared with only one thing in mind...that is that, at all times, it should be the best advice for you, the client...no equivocation, no conflict of interest, simply the best advice for you.

Our intention is to be there at every stage, to help navigate and, hopefully, avoid the various pitfalls which are out there. From realistic, pre sales valuations, to advice on property marketing and how to maximise...and handle...viewings. From how to achieve the best possible price, whilst being very open, fair and transparent with all interested parties to advice on surveys, mortgages and conveyancing...we can even, if asked, express an opinion on any property in which you are interested.

Our aim is simply to do everything in our power to help you make your move with as few complications as possible...and to do so at a very reasonable cost. It's all part of the service!

Introduction

Being transparent and providing accessible information to individuals about how we may use personal data is a key element of the Data Protection Act (DPA) ant the EU General Data Protection Regulations (GDPR).

Privilege Properties is committed to building trust and confidence in our ability to process your personal information.

Consent

Privilege Properties will always try to inform you of what/ when/why information we require. Privilege Properties forms several relationships with Individuals to include:

  • Viewer
  • Vendor
  • Purchaser
  • Landlord
  • Tenant

The information we collect and hold about you will be dependent on your relationship with us. Generally, we do not rely on consent as a lawful basis for processing your personal data for the purposes you have requested. When you provide us with the prerequisite information, Privilege Properties will only use that information for the purposes intended. For example,

  • Arranging a viewing of a rental / For sale property
  • Letting Your Property
  • Selling Your Property
  • The process involved in the purchasing of a for sale Property
  • The process involved in the purchasing a Rental Property

For us to complete any of the above transactions we would request (but not limited to)

  • Your Full name
  • Address
  • Mobile number
  • Email address

If your relationship grows with Privilege Properties and we build upon our services, we may require further information such as Within the sales section of the business we may ask

  • Status of your own property
  • Would you like a valuation on your own property?
  • Family, probate or inheritance details
  • Certain features regarding your own property
  • Can we help you with a mortgage referral?
  • Mortgage or Financial Information
  • Would you like to go onto our mailing list to receive information about suitable new listings? This service can be terminated at any time
  • We will retain the sold price on properties for compariable evidence

Within the rental section of the business if you wish to apply for one of our properties there is an application that is required to be completed. This can entail a Credit Check on behalf of our landlords, therefore we need to send information to them to the company to complete this.

This will ask for further information with regards to (but not limited to)

  • Your employment Status
  • Your current residential status and possibly current landlord’s details
  • Do you have any CCJs or Bankruptcy?
  • Do you smoke or have any pets?
  • Next of Kin details

Privilege Properties often requires liaising with several third parties’ professionals / businesses, who provide essential assistance to complete services that we are engaged upon with Individual clients.

These include:

  • EPC Surveyors (Carry out legally required Energy Performance cert)
  • Solicitors (For both Purchaser and vendor)
  • Mortgage Providers (to keep up to date with the status of Mortgage applications)
  • Surveyors (Assessing properties on behalf of Mortgage Providers)
  • HMRC (Legal obligation under The Money Laundering regulations & associated legislation/Rental incomes)
  • Credit Reference Agencies (In relation to credit Checks for tenants)
  • NIHE (In reference to tenant’s application for housing Benefit)
  • Professional Contractors (Manage and maintain rental properties on Landlords behalf)

We may need to share information with these organizations for more than one reason, not all your personal information will be required every time.

We aim to minimize the personal information shared and the instances of sharing to what is needed for the specific purpose in line with the Data Protection Act. Under the Money Laundering Regulations 2007, we have an obligation to secure information regarding Identity and funding. Under these regulations and associated legislation we have a legal obligation to advise the appropriate bodies if we have concerns regarding any transactions.

We must also keep all information on file for 7 years.

What rights do you have?

As a Data subject you have the rights to:

  • Access to your Personal information
  • Confirmation that your personal information is being processed
  • Amend any information that is inaccurate or incomplete

There are certain circumstances where information can be withdrawn. These include:

  • When personal information is no longer necessary
  • An objection in the process has occurred & there is no legitimate interest in continuing (For example, Withdrawal of your offer on a property)
  • When Consent has been withdrawn

Retention of records

  • All hard copy data is held in a locked secure location
  • All data received by email or other electronic means is kept secure, using a variety of security and antivirus software systems
  • We use a Credible waste management disposal company to dispose/ destroy our hard copy of data when appropriate
